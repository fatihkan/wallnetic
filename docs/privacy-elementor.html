Privacy Policy

Wallnetic - Live Video Wallpapers for macOS

Effective Date: March 2026

Your Privacy is Protected

Wallnetic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your wallpapers and preferences stay entirely on your device.

Introduction

Wallnetic ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our macOS application.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Wallnetic is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No crash reports sent to external servers
  • No cookies or identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Local Data Storage

Wallnetic stores the following data locally on your device only:

Video Files

Wallpaper videos you import are stored in the app's sandbox folder:
~/Library/Application Support/Wallnetic/Library

App Preferences

Your settings are stored locally using macOS standard preferences:

  • Theme preference (Dark/Light/System)
  • Playback settings (pause on battery, pause on fullscreen)
  • Launch at login preference
  • Per-display wallpaper assignments
  • Last selected wallpaper

Collections & Favorites

Your wallpaper collections and favorites are stored locally and sync via iCloud if enabled on your Mac.

Thumbnail Cache

Video thumbnails are cached locally for faster loading. This cache can be cleared anytime.

Important: This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Network Access

Wallnetic operates entirely offline. The current version does not require an internet connection to function and does not communicate with any external servers.

Note: Future versions may include optional AI features that require internet access. These features will be opt-in and clearly disclosed.

App Permissions

Wallnetic requests the following macOS permissions:

Permission Purpose
File Access To import and play video files you select
Screen Access To display wallpapers on your desktop
Login Items Optional - to launch at system startup

Third-Party Services

Wallnetic does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Your experience is completely private.

Children's Privacy

Wallnetic does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages and is rated 4+ on the App Store.

Data Security

Your data is protected by macOS security features:

  • App Sandbox: Wallnetic runs in a sandboxed environment, isolated from other apps
  • Secure Storage: All data is stored using macOS secure storage mechanisms
  • No Transmission: Your data is never sent over the internet

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
